Joy in Summary

I am a lawyer/peace practitioner, writer, spiritual seeker, traveller and homemaker. Along my spiritual journey, I have given myself more names- so my full names are now Joy Precious Stunning Diamond Kagwiria Mbaabu. My friends and colleagues simply call me JP. It is a wonderful and humbling feeling to give myself a personal identity, over and above the names my parents gave me. I have carefully selected each of my new names, and doubtlessly, each carries a profound meaning to me. I am who l am, not what I do- simply, I am a human being, not a human doing. What l do is meant to keep me busy and contribute to making this world more fun and fair place to live.
