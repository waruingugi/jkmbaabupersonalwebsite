The Homemaker

Of all blessings, that of a family stands out. I am grateful to the family into which l was born. I am grateful to the family I was married into. I am grateful for the family God has enabled me to make with my dear husband. I am grateful for family as source of faithfulness and fruitfulness. I am grateful that our family is ever expanding, with new blessings pursuing us year after year. I strive to make my home a haven of love, peace, understanding, compassion and freedom (with responsibility).
